# B1 Chapter 3
## Summary  
  
Following his awakening, Seth visits Marcus to treat the wounds he suffered from the Tempest Hare and seeks advice on how to progress as a Primalist. Marcus confirms the identity of the beast, warns Seth about its danger, and gives him a gift left behind by his father: a Beast Encyclopedia intended to help him survive as a Primalist.  
  
After meeting Mael, Seth remembers the monthly tax collection and rushes home. The tax collector discovers that Seth has awakened and demands payment of the Awakening Tax. When Seth cannot pay the full amount, his house is confiscated. In an apparent attempt to impress unseen observers, the tax collector burns the last portrait of Seth's parents and gives him three months to pay the remaining debt before being sentenced to forced labor.  
  
## Major Events  
  
- Seth visits Marcus after awakening.  
- Marcus identifies the beast that attacked Seth as a Tempest Hare.  
- Marcus gives Seth a Beast Encyclopedia left by his father.  
- Seth meets Mael in town.  
- The tax collector discovers Seth has awakened.  
- Seth is forced to pay the Awakening Tax.  
- His home is confiscated by House Faertis.  
- The portrait of Seth's parents is destroyed.  
- Seth receives a three-month deadline to pay the remaining debt.

## Concepts Introduced  
  
### Tempest Hare  
  
- Arcane Beast specializing in wind-based spells.  
- Possesses extremely high Arcane Power.  
- Considered highly dangerous for unawakened individuals.  
  
### Beast Encyclopedia  
  
- A reference book containing information about [[Arcane Beasts]].  
- Left behind by Seth's father.  
- Intended to help Seth survive and progress as a [[Primalist]].  
  
### Awakening Tax  
  
- Tax imposed on newly awakened Wielders.  
- Calculated according to the average number of Awakening Stones normally required.  
- Must be paid regardless of the actual number of stones used.
