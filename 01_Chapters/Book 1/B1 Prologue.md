# Prologue

## Summary

Following the disastrous failure of a coup against the King of Kastal, several nobles and military leaders gather in secret to assess the situation. Their army of three thousand Silver-Tier Wielders was annihilated inside the Silver Sky Rift by a mysterious Platinum-Tier warrior from Draeria.

During the meeting, a Black Reaper assassin appears and offers to investigate the warrior's identity in exchange for two future services: the assassination of Jaeda, head of Kastal's Adventurers Guild, and assistance in a future operation inside a Temporary Rift. Unable to refuse, the conspirators agree and sign a soul contract with him.

---

## Major Events

- The failure of the coup against the King of [[Kastal Kingdom|Kastal]] is revealed.
    
- Three thousand [[Silver Tier]] soldiers are killed.
    
- A mysterious Draerian [[Platinum Tier]] Wielder is discussed.
    
- The existence of a powerful contracted beast accompanying the Draerian is revealed.
    
- The conspirators decide to conceal their involvement from the King.
    
- A Black Reaper assassin appears before the conspirators.
    
- A deal is negotiated between the Black Reaper and the coup leaders.
    
- A soul contract is accepted by all participants.
