# B2 Chapter 2

## Summary

After trading the mysterious flower for an Awakening Stone and an Identify scroll, Seth returns home and successfully awakens as a Wielder despite having only a single stone.

After awakening, he learns the Identify spell and discovers his Status Window. To his disappointment, he awakens as a Primalist instead of the Warrior he had spent years trying to become.

Despite his frustration, Seth resolves to move forward and find a way to achieve his goals.

## World Information Revealed

### Awakening

Awakening requires igniting an Aether Well by channeling aether from an Awakening Stone.

Most people require multiple Awakening Stones before succeeding.

### Path

A person's Path heavily influences the Class obtained during awakening.

### Spell-scrolls

Spell-scrolls can directly engrave spell grooves into a Wielder, bypassing the normal learning process.

### Status Window

The Status Window displays Class, Rank, Stats, and known Spells.

### Primalists

Primalists are considered one of the most dangerous Classes because their progression heavily rewards risk-taking and dangerous situations.
