# B1 Chapter 1

## Summary

Nine years after the events of the prologue, [[Seth]] is hunting near the outskirts of the Wicked Forest in an attempt to earn enough money to survive under the increasingly oppressive taxation of House Faertis.

During the hunt he discovers a mysterious aether-saturated flower guarded by a powerful arcane hare. Despite the danger, he manages to harvest the flower and narrowly escapes after the beast attacks him with devastating wind-based spells.

Returning to Sunatown, Seth witnesses the worsening conditions of the population under House Faertis and becomes even more determined to awaken as a Wielder. The chapter ends as he approaches a Wandering Merchant's stall to examine an Awakening Stone.

## Major Events

- Seth discovers an aether-saturated flower.
- First encounter with an Arcane Beast.
- Introduction of a powerful silver hare.
- Seth successfully harvests the flower.
- Seth survives a chase by the hare.
- The economic decline of Sunatown is shown.
- The oppression of House Faertis is explained.
- Introduction of Awakening Stones.
- Seth reaches the age required to awaken.
