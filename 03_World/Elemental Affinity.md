# Elemental Affinity

## Overview
Elemental Affinity represents a Wielder's connection and compatibility with a specific element.
Unlike Basic Stats, Affinity is measured using the Tier and Subtier system.

---
## Development
Elemental Affinity can be improved through multiple methods:
### Natural Development
- Training
- Repeated spell usage
- Natural talent
### Environmental Exposure
- Exposure to [[Essences]]
- Spending time in environments saturated with elemental Aether
### Resources
- Consumption of elemental [[Seeds]]
- Use of resources that improve [[Aether]] sensitivity
### External Enhancements
- Use of enhancers
- Certain [[Legacies]]

Developing Affinity becomes progressively harder at higher levels, often requiring increasingly valuable resources or prolonged exposure to the associated element.

---
## Effects
A higher Affinity generally:

- Increases spell potency
- Reduces spell costs
- Improves control
- Facilitates learning elemental spells
- Unlocks certain subclasses

---
## Affinity Limits
Most Wielders possess a single elemental Affinity.
Some possess two.
Developing multiple Affinities simultaneously is possible but requires substantially more resources.

---
## Arcane Beasts
Arcane Beasts are often born with natural elemental Affinities related to their species.
