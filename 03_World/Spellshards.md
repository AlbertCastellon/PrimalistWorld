# Spellshards

## Overview
## Overview
Spellshards are a special type of [[Beaststones]] that contain the engraved grooves of a spell. They allow Wielders to learn spells directly and are among the most valuable resources obtained from Arcane Beasts.
They are found more commonly within [[Rifts]] due to the higher concentration of Aether.
## Formation
Spellshards appear when beasts learn spells outside their innate ones, these powerful beasts leave the grooves of that spell on their beaststone, making it a spellshard.
## Mastery
The spell learned from a Spellshard inherits a level of mastery based on the grooves engraved within the Spellshard itself.
Higher-quality Spellshards may grant spells with greater initial mastery, reducing the time needed to refine the spell.
## Uses
### Learn Spells
Can be used to learn the spell directly
### Scholars
Can be given to a [[Scholar]]. Scholars are able to use them to create a number of scrolls, though they may also end up with nothing.
The number of scrolls produced, as well as the probability of failure, is influenced more by luck than by the Scholar's ability.
These scrolls can teach a Wielder the spell just like the original Spellshard.
Scholars may also uncover some of the spell's mysteries, eventually allowing them to create scrolls of that spell without requiring a Spellshard, provided they possess the necessary materials.
## Legacy Spellshards
Legacy Spellshards are an extremely rare type of Spellshard created by exceptionally powerful Wielders.
Unlike ordinary Spellshards, they do not merely grant a spell. When fused with a compatible Wielder, they form a Core and grant a Legacy, allowing the recipient to follow the Path of the original owner.
For more information see [[Legacies]].
## Importance
They are one of the primary methods of learning new spells for those that can't afford spell scrolls, and one of the few ways to acquire high-Tier spells.
This makes them extremely valuable, especially in nations where powerful spells are tightly controlled by nobles, governments, or influential organizations.
Not every beast leaves behind a Spellshard, making them considerably rarer than ordinary Beaststones.
