# Frigid Tulip of the Astral Ice Cave

## Description

A raw tulip that has absorbed a tremendous amount of the coldness of its surroundings.

---

## Effects

- Increases Arcane Power by 5 to 10
- Increases Arcane Power by 4 to 8%.
- Reduces the cooldown of Ice spells of the Copper / Iron / Silver Tier by 25%.
