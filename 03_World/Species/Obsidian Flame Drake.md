# Obsidian Flame Drake

## Species Type
Drake

---
## Appearance
The Obsidian Flame Drake is a massive, twenty-foot-long reptilian beast covered in jagged obsidian-black scales threaded with glowing orange veins resembling cooling magma. Thick plumes of steam escape from its nostrils, and the heat radiating from its body scorches the ground beneath its claws.
