# Fishlord

## Species Type
Humanoids

---
## Appearance
Fishlords are fish-like humanoid [[Arcane Beasts]] standing between four and seven feet tall. They possess green, scaled skin, long gangly limbs ending in hefty claws, numerous fins across their bodies, and a horizontal head dominated by a large maw filled with multiple rows of razor-sharp teeth. Their large, bulbous eyes and the prominent fin on top of their head are their most distinctive features.
