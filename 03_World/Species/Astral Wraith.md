# Astral Wraith

## Species Type
Spirit

---
## Appearance
Astral Wraiths are humanoid beings composed entirely of swirling, luminous blue aether that crackles with an electric hum. Their weightless bodies lack flesh and bone, consisting solely of concentrated energy capable of reshaping into crystalline weapons or other constructs. Their ethereal forms bear a resemblance to Spirits, though they are formed from condensed aether rather than drifting smoke.
