## Appearance
The Glacial Shield-Horntail is a massive quadrupedal beast encased in thick, translucent layers of ice that serve as natural armor. Its broad head is protected by a shield-like cranial plate from which three crystalline horns emerge, each glowing with a frigid inner light. Its powerful tail ends in a large, spiked club of solid ice.
