# Water Elemental

## Species Type
Elemental

---
## Appearance
A humanoid elemental formed from translucent, constantly swirling water. Unlike Earth Elementals, its body is fully formed, with attached limbs and head. It glides smoothly across the ground, leaving trails of water in its wake.
