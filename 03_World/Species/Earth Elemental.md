## Appearance
A hulking humanoid construct formed from granite and obsidian. Its crude torso is an amalgam of floating boulders, with a perfectly spherical stone serving as its head. Two massive rock arms hover beside its body, connected by shimmering strands of [[Aether]], while its lower body floats just above the ground.
