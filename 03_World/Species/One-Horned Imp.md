# One-Horned Imp

## Species Type
Demon

---
## Appearance
The One-Horned Imp is a small, winged humanoid beast with molten-red skin and a single twisted horn protruding from its forehead. Its leathery wings allow it to hover effortlessly, while its three-clawed hands wield a golden trident often shrouded in crackling dark aether. Despite its diminutive size, it radiates an overwhelming aura of power and death.
