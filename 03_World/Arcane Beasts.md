# Arcane Beasts

## Overview
Arcane Beasts are creatures capable of sensing, manipulating, and utilizing [[Aether]].
Unlike ordinary animals, they possess an Aether Well and are able to grow stronger through [[Progression]], develop affinities, learn spells, and advance through Tiers.
They are among the most common inhabitants of [[Rifts]] and represent one of the greatest threats faced by Wielders.

---
## Progression
Like Wielders, Arcane Beasts possess:

- Stats
- Tiers
- Ranks
- Affinities
- A Path

Their power generally increases as they progress through higher Tiers.

---
## Potential
Every Arcane Beast species possesses a natural Potential Tier.
This represents the Tier that members of that species can normally reach without extraordinary opportunities.

Examples:
- [[White Fox]] → Copper Potential
- [[Inferno Bear]] → Iron Potential
- [[Tenebrous Direwolf]] → Silver Potential

Some exceptional individuals may exceed their species' natural potential through special circumstances.

---
## Affinities
Most Arcane Beasts possess one or more [[Elemental Affinity|Elemental Affinities]].
These affinities influence:

- Spell development
- Evolution
- Path
- Behavior

The affinity of an individual beast may differ slightly from the average affinity of its species.

---
## Spells
Arcane Beasts can develop spells naturally through instinct and necessity.
Unlike most Wielders, they rarely rely on scrolls and instead engrave spell grooves through repeated use of Aether.
Some beasts possess Innate Spells from birth, while others develop new spells throughout their lives.

---
## Beaststones
Upon death, Arcane Beasts may leave behind a [[Beaststones]].
These crystallized concentrations of Aether are valuable resources used in crafting, artificing, and magical research.

---
## Spellshards
When an Arcane Beast successfully learns and engraves a spell, traces of those grooves may remain within its Beaststone after death.
These specialized Beaststones become [[Spellshards]], allowing Wielders to learn the same spell.

---
## Relationship with Rifts
Arcane Beasts are most commonly found within [[Rifts]], where the higher concentration of Aether accelerates their growth and evolution.
Many of the strongest known Arcane Beasts originate from powerful or Overgrown Rifts.

---

Arcane Beasts can be classified according to several criteria:
