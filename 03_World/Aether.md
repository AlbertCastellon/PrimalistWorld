# Aether

## Overview
Aether is the fundamental energy that permeates the world and serves as the foundation of all supernatural phenomena. It exists naturally within the environment, arcane beasts, and wielders, allowing them to manipulate reality through spells, enhance their bodies, and advance along their Paths.
Without Aether, neither magical abilities nor the growth of wielders would be possible.

---
## Nature of Aether
Aether is both a source of power and a catalyst for evolution.
It naturally accumulates in living beings and can be shaped through training, affinities, spells, cores, and legacies. The amount and quality of Aether a being can control largely determines its overall strength.
Different regions contain different concentrations of Aether, with [[Rifts]] often possessing significantly denser Aether than the outside world.

---
## Aether Wells
Every wielder possesses an internal reservoir known as a **Well**. The Well stores Aether and determines how much energy can be used before exhaustion occurs.
The size of a Well is represented by the **Well Capacity** attribute. Its unities are called uniums.
When a wielder casts spells or activates certain abilities, Aether is consumed from the Well.
Aether naturally regenerates over time, with regeneration speed being influenced by the **Regeneration** attribute, Seeds, spells, and other effects.

---
## Spellcasting
Aether is the primary fuel required for spellcasting.
When casting a spell, Aether flows through the spell's internal structure and is transformed into a specific effect.
The amount of Aether consumed depends on factors such as:

- Spell Tier
- Spell Quality
- Spell Mastery
- Affinities
- Modifications made to the spell

More powerful spells generally require greater amounts of Aether.

---
## Body Enhancement
Aether can also reinforce the physical body.
Even without directly casting a spell, wielders continuously circulate Aether through their bodies, increasing:

- Strength
- Toughness
- Agility
- Healing

This is one of the primary reasons why higher-ranked individuals possess superhuman physical capabilities.

---

## Affinities
Aether can be aligned with specific concepts or elements known as **Affinities**.
Common examples include:

- Earth
- Ice
- Darkness
- Poison
- Null

An Affinity increases a wielder's compatibility with related spells and often improves the efficiency and potency of Aether used for those effects.
Affinities themselves can develop and grow stronger over time.

---

## Aether and Advancement
Aether plays a critical role in Rank [[Progression]].
As wielders improve their manipulation and sensing abilities, and strengthen their cores, their relationship with Aether deepens.
Higher Ranks allow greater quantities of Aether to be controlled and utilized.
This increased control contributes directly to improved attributes and stronger spellcasting.

---
## Aether in Arcane Beasts
Arcane beasts possess Aether just as wielders do.
Their bodies naturally absorb and refine it, allowing them to develop:

- Magical abilities
- Affinities
- Enhanced physical capabilities
- Evolutionary growth

Many beast species develop unique biological adaptations shaped by Aether.

---

## Environmental Aether
Aether is present throughout the world in varying concentrations.
Areas with unusually dense Aether can give rise to, or be caused by:
- [[Rifts]]
- [[Essences]]
- [[Seeds]]
- Rare resources
- Unique beast populations
The environmental composition of Aether often influences the Affinities found within a region.
For example, a Rift saturated with Ice Aether may contain Ice-affinity beasts, Seeds, and materials.

---

## Relationship with Seeds and Legacies
Seeds and [[Legacies]] modify how a wielder interacts with Aether.
Seeds often improve:

- Aether efficiency
- Spell potency
- Attribute growth
- Affinity specialization

Legacies can fundamentally alter the way Aether is perceived, manipulated, or integrated into a wielder's [[Path]].
Some Legacies grant abilities that would otherwise be impossible through normal spellcasting.

---

## Notes

- Aether is the foundation of all known magical systems.
- Nearly every form of supernatural power ultimately relies on Aether.
- Mastery over Aether is one of the defining factors separating weak wielders from powerful ones.
