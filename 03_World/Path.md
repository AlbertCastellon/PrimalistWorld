# Path
## Overview
The Path is the combination of talent, destiny and personal choices. It guides the wielders, while it is manipulated by them through the choices they make and the way they choose to live.

## Classes and Subclasses
When a person awakens and becomes a Wielder, he becomes a class based on his talents and the training they have been doing until this point but also based on their Path, for example his way of thinking and how have they been living, for example people who have been hunting since young usually become [[Primalist]], if they are training like [[Warrior]] using different weapons they normally become Warriors. 
During their life it will also shape partially the increase of stats and their distribution, for someone who wishes to become a shield for their friends, they will see easier to increase their Toughness, and someone who wishes to kill or get revenge they will have it easier to improve stats that boost their offensive power.
It also impacts on the Subclass one awakens, through the stats spread and the will the Wielder possesses.

## Evolution
The Path can evolve and change through the life of the Wielder. Major life events, personal revelations, traumatic experiences, and long-term changes in goals or beliefs can gradually reshape a Wielder's Path.
A Legacy allows a Wielder to walk a Path previously forged by another.By accepting a Legacy, a Wielder partially adopts the worldview, instincts, and tendencies of its original owner. Because of that that they're more impactful the weaker the Wielder is.
