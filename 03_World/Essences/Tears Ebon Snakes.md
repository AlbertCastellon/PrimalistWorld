# Tears of Ebon Snakes

## Description

Distilled tears from Ebon Snakes, saturated with [[Darkness]] aether.

Prolonged exposure can:
- deepen [[Darkness]] affinity,
- improve Darkness-based spells,
- accelerate growth and Regeneration of Darkness-affinity beasts.

---

## Effects

- Enhances [[Darkness]] affinity training.
- Improves Darkness spell development.
- Accelerates growth of Darkness-affinity beasts.
- Increases Regeneration of Darkness-affinity beasts.
