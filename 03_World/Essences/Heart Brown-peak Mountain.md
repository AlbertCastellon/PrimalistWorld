# Heart of Brown-Peak Mountain

## Description

A concentrated nexus of ambient [[Earth]] aether.

Prolonged exposure can:
- deepen [[Earth]] affinity,
- assist improvement of Earth-based spells,
- accelerate growth and Regeneration of Earth-affinity beasts.

---

## Effects

- Enhances Earth-affinity training.
- Improves Earth spell development.
- Accelerates growth of Earth-affinity beasts.
- Improves Regeneration of Earth-affinity beasts.
