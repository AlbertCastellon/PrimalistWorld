# Legacies

## Overview
A Legacy is a special inheritance left behind by an exceptionally powerful Wielder. It takes the form of a scalable spell and a Core that guide the recipient toward a specific [[Path]], influencing their growth, abilities, and even aspects of their personality.

---
## Origin
Legacies are formed when extremely powerful Wielders die (not specified the exact level needed). From their bodies are obtained a number of spellshards that each give a different spell from the Wielder. This allows more than one person to be able to obtain a Legacy from the same person.

---
## Advantages
The legacy other than granting a powerful scalable spell, also forms a core in the Wielders chest that can provide some help like enhanced instincts or higher affinity towards an element. It also allows for easier breakthroughs for soft caps and tier caps, since the recipient is following a [[Path]] already proven. Many Legacies also grant access to rare or unique Subclasses that would otherwise be impossible to obtain.

---
## Known Legacies

### Kreytorus' Legacy
Legacy for Primalists, assumed to come from a Draerian [[Primalist]], related to the bond of Arcane Beasts and combat with them.
Known user: [[Seth]]
### Friora Milleria's Legacy
Legacy for Elemetalists, it originates from a powerful but unknown [[Elementalist]], related to the use and control of [[Ice]]. It is called **Ice Kingdom**
Known user: [[Elena]]
### Falcaru Santori's Legacy
Legacy for Guardians and Warriors, originated from a [[Light Crusader]] that governed the Bridan Empire for almost a century and half-stepped into godhood, there are 6 known spellshards.
