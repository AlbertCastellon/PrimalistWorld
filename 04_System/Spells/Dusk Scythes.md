# Dusk Scythes

## Description

Summons blades of condensed darkness around the user's forearms, forming lethal close-combat shadow weapons.

---

## Scaling Effects

| Tier | Effects |
|---|---|
| Iron | Summons shadow blades for 30 seconds. Ignores 20 Toughness + 15% of the target's Toughness. Consumes 150 uniums. |
| Silver | Summons stronger shadow blades for 30 seconds. Ignores 50 Toughness + 15% of the target's Toughness. Consumes 350 uniums. |

### Additional Scaling

- Potency scales with:
  - Arcane Power
  - Darkness Affinity
  - Spell Quality

- Duration increases according to:
  - Spell Quality
  - Darkness Affinity

- Cooldown decreases according to:
  - Spell Quality
  - Darkness Affinity

Cooldown: 5 minutes.
