# Armored Phantom Fists

## Description
A fusion spell combining the defensive properties of Iron Fists with the armor-breaking power of Phantom Punch. The user's arms become encased in undead-infused stone, greatly enhancing both offense and defense.

---

## Effects
Increases Strength by 40 and ignores 15% of the target's Toughness for 30 seconds. Increases Toughness by 10 + 5%. Potency increases by 100% against barriers and shielding spells. Consumes 120 uniums.
