# Beastmaster Rage

## Description
An evolved version of Primal Rage that extends its effects to the Wielder's contracted beasts. The spell empowers both master and companions simultaneously, reflecting the Beastmaster Path's focus on shared growth and combat synergy.

---

## Effects
Increases Strength and Agility of the owner and their beasts by 30 for 7 minutes. Decreases the owner's Toughness by 30 for 25 minutes afterward. Duration increases according to spell quality. Consumes 120 uniums over the duration. Cooldown: 8 hours.
