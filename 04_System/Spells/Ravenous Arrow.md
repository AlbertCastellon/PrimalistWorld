# Ravenous Arrow

## Description
Conjures an aether-forged bow in the user's hands, allowing the creation of specialized arrows that consume surrounding Aether upon impact. The spell is particularly effective against barriers and defensive spells.

---

## Scaling Effects
| Tier   | Effects                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         |
| ------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Iron   | Summons an aether-forged bow for 2 minutes. Arrows scale with Arcane Power, Null Affinity, and spell quality. Ignores 50 Toughness + 30% of the target's Toughness. Deals 200% increased potency against barriers and shielding spells. Consumes 60 uniums to summon and 30 uniums per arrow.   |
| Silver | Summons an aether-forged bow for 2 minutes. Arrows scale with Arcane Power, Null Affinity, and spell quality. Ignores 120 Toughness + 30% of the target's Toughness. Deals 200% increased potency against barriers and shielding spells. Consumes 100 uniums to summon and 50 uniums per arrow. |
